The **AI Prompt Coach** teaches beginners and everyday users how to write clear, effective prompts for AI tools like ChatGPT—without jargon, programming, or trial-and-error frustration.
Most frustration with AI does not come from the technology itself. It comes from unclear instructions. When prompts are vague, AI responses are vague. **Clear prompts produce clear results.**

## What Is the AI Prompt Coach?

The **AI Prompt Coach** is a custom ChatGPT-based assistant designed to help you learn *how to ask better questions* so you get better answers from AI.
Instead of simply responding to prompts, the Coach explains why a prompt works or fails, helps you rewrite it, and guides you toward clearer, more effective wording.

* Understand how AI interprets instructions
* Fix vague or ineffective prompts
* Write prompts for learning, writing, work, or creativity
* Reduce hallucinations and irrelevant answers
* Build confidence using AI responsibly

## How to Use the AI Prompt Coach

### Step 1: Describe Your Goal

Tell the Coach what you want to accomplish. You do not need perfect wording.

* “Help me write a better AI prompt.”
* “Why didn’t my ChatGPT prompt work?”
* “Explain a complex topic for beginners.”
* “Rewrite this prompt to get clearer results.”

### Step 2: Follow the Guidance

The Coach will ask clarifying questions, suggest improvements, and explain the logic behind each revision.

### Step 3: Refine and Reuse

Over time, you will learn how to write effective prompts on your own and reduce wasted time correcting AI output.
