## Author Bio (Copy/Paste)

### Short Bio (75 words)

Grant Ingraham is an engineer, author, and lifelong technical communicator who has spent more than five decades
explaining complex technical subjects to people of all ages. He has written hundreds of consumer manuals and helped
business leaders, judges, engineers, and the general public understand science and technology with clarity. Retired
now, Grant has turned his expertise toward artificial intelligence. His latest book, *AI Prompts*, teaches everyone
how to use AI safely, confidently, and productively.

### Long Bio (182 words)

**Grant Ingraham is an engineer, author, and veteran technical communicator who has spent more than 50 years translating
complex ideas into clear, practical guidance for ordinary people. Since 1973, his work has focused on helping non-technical
audiences understand subjects that often feel intimidating—from writing hundreds of owner’s manuals for consumer products
to explaining physics, engineering, and safety concepts to audiences everywhere.**

His career includes consulting with federal, state, local, and international agencies such as the CPSC, NFPA, and USPTO;
educating business leaders, attorneys, judges, and engineers; and writing standards and instructional materials used across
industries. In the computer field, Grant programmed, maintained, operated, and sold a wide range of computing systems,
developing complete software packages for both businesses and individuals while continually teaching users how to get the most
from their technology.

Since retiring in 2012, Grant has devoted himself to studying, using, and writing about artificial intelligence. His latest book,
*AI Prompts*, serves as a beginner’s guide that walks readers from their very first interaction with ChatGPT to confident,
safe, and productive AI use.

Today, Grant is committed to correcting misinformation about AI and helping everyday people understand it as what it truly is:
**a powerful tool that must be understood to be used well.**

*Note for hosts/interviewers:* Grant is new to promoting his books through media appearances, but brings decades of professional
public speaking and instructional experience.

### AI Prompts - Master ChatGPT Fast With Easy Prompts For Home, Work, And Creativity

**Format:** 168 pages, Paperback |
**Publication Date:** December 8, 2025 |
**ISBN:** 979-8274069939

*AI Prompts* is a practical, beginner-friendly guide designed to help everyday users understand how to communicate
effectively with artificial intelligence tools such as ChatGPT. Written by Grant Ingraham, an engineer and lifelong educator,
the book cuts through technical jargon and hype to explain what prompts are, why they matter, and how small changes in wording can
dramatically improve AI responses. Using clear examples, real-world scenarios, and step-by-step techniques, *AI Prompts* empowers
readers—especially seniors, parents, educators, and first-time AI users—to use AI confidently, responsibly, and productively at home,
at work, and in creative pursuits, without needing any technical background.

#### Key Takeaways

* **The quality of an AI’s output depends directly on the quality of the prompt.** Clear, specific, and well-structured prompts consistently produce more useful, accurate, and relevant results than vague or casual requests.
* **You do not need technical skills to use AI effectively.** Anyone can achieve strong results by learning a few practical prompt patterns that guide AI behavior, tone, and level of detail.
* **Context is critical.** Providing background information, goals, and constraints helps AI respond in ways that are aligned with real-world needs rather than generic answers.
* **AI is a tool, not an authority.** The book emphasizes reviewing, questioning, and verifying AI outputs, reinforcing responsible use and human judgment.
* **Good prompts improve productivity across everyday tasks.** From writing, learning, and planning to problem-solving and creativity, thoughtful prompting saves time and reduces frustration in daily life.

#### Ideal For

*AI Prompts* is ideal for beginners, seniors, parents, educators, and everyday users who want to understand and use
artificial intelligence confidently, responsibly, and effectively—without technical jargon or prior experience.

### Cybersecurity: How To Be Secure – Browsing, Banking, Social Media, Shopping

**Format:** 134 pages, Paperback and Kindle |
**Publication Date:** July 31, 2025 |
**ISBN:** 979-8272964557

*Cybersecurity: How To Be Secure – Browsing, Banking, Social Media, Shopping* is a practical, plain-language guide written for
everyday users who want to stay safe online without becoming technical experts. Drawing on decades of experience explaining complex
technology to non-technical audiences, Grant Ingraham breaks down modern cybersecurity risks into clear, understandable concepts and
realistic habits anyone can follow. The book focuses on real-world threats people actually face—email scams, unsafe websites, weak
passwords, online banking risks, and social media exposure—and explains what to do, what to avoid, and why it matters.

#### Key Takeaways

* **Cybersecurity is about habits, not fear.** Small, consistent actions dramatically reduce risk.
* **Most attacks target people, not computers.** Understanding scams and manipulation is critical.
* **You don’t need technical expertise to stay safe online.** Awareness and common sense go a long way.
* **Everyday activities carry different levels of risk.** Browsing, banking, email, and social media require different precautions.
* **Good security improves confidence.** Knowing what to do reduces anxiety and confusion.

#### Ideal For

This book is ideal for adults 55+, beginners, parents, families, and non-technical users who want clear, trustworthy guidance on staying
safe online without jargon, fear-based messaging, or technical overwhelm.
