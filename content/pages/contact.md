### Before You Email — Please Read

* **State your main point in the first sentence.**
* **If requesting an ARC file**, mention your preferred format (EPUB or PDF).
* **Use your real name and a working email address.**
* **No attachments or links**—these will not be accepted.
* **No advertising or unsolicited pitches**—these will be ignored.
* **Please allow up to 24 hours for a reply.**
